Welcome to my site!


I am a beginner coder/ hacker that is beginning the process of learning and figuring everything out. I learned the basics of HTML in Highschool and I am learning everything about web app pentesting on TryHackMe.com. I have learned a lot of things from TryHackMe! One of my favorite "Hacktivities" to do is the CTFs (Capture The Flag). The cool thing about CTFs is, you get to run a virtual machine that you can hack into legally, basically allowing you to simulate being an attacker on a system. I run a distribution of Linux called Kali on a raspberry pi 5 and I use tools that professional hackers use in the real world. THM also offers a more competitive mode of hacking called King of the Hill, which I haven't done, but sounds super fun. If you do decide to try out TryHackMe, please don't hesitate to go to the Writeups tab to find walkthroughs for some of the rooms that I have completed.

* * *

My end goal is to become a red team penetration tester. A red team member acts as the attacker in a simulated attack on a system. They take on the role as a bad actor (without malicious intent) to find any holes in an organizations systems and report on their findings to assist the organization in being more secure. On the flip side, a blue team member takes on the role of a defender of an organization. They ensure the CIA (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availibility) triad remains intact. They also make sure the organization remains GRC compliant, as well as watch the network for any suspicious activity. Both of these roles do alot more than I listed above, but I am still learning more about this industry.
